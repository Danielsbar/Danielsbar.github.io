Daniel Sbar's Data Analytics Portfolio

I am Daniel J. Sbar, I have experience working in Python, JavaScript, SQL and Tableau.
I mostly enjoy work that requires collaboration of ideas and expertise as well as projects that allow me to problem solve, come up with unique solutions, and engage in difficult mathematics..

I am most proud of the work I was able to do with the Bike Sharing data, I would recommend checking it out and playing with the interactive model. Having used Python to manipulate the data from Kaggle into Tableau, I had a lot of information to work with and was able to display a lot more interesting techniques. The other two projects, I pulled from The Bureau of Economic Analysis and The Energy Information Administration into SQL and was able to clean and manipulate the data there.

Bike Share

Exploring the effectiveness of London's Bike Share program from April 2015 until March of 2017 and how weather affects riders usage.

Natural Land Usage

Evaluating the economic impact of Recreational use of Natural Land and the economic impact of using land for Resource Extraction.

Energy Costs

Understanding the upfront and long term costs of various forms of energy to indentify how various energy sources may suit the needs of different groups.
